Red Rose

Rosa Kerria · The Eternal Symbol

SYMBOLISM & MEANING

The Red Rose is the ultimate botanical expression of love, passion, and deep longing. It conveys a message of affection and courage.

Historically, the red rose has held a place of reverence across cultures.
