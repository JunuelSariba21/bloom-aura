Red Rose

Rosa rubiginosa

Red roses symbolize love and passion. Many types exist, each with unique beauty. Classic red roses like ‘Mr. Lincoln’ offer strong fragrance and tall growth. Modern red roses like ‘Freedom’ have better disease resistance and bloom often.
