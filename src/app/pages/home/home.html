Red Rose

Rosa rubiginosa

Red roses symbolize profound love and deep passion. Throughout history, they have been the ultimate symbol of romance, courage, and beauty.

White Lily

Lilium candidum

White lilies symbolize purity, rebirth, and majesty. Often associated with royalty and elegant new beginnings, their striking petals capture the light beautifully.

Yellow Tulip

Tulipa sylvestris

Yellow tulips convey cheerful thoughts and sunshine. They are the perfect bloom to express joy, friendship, and positive energy to someone you care about.

Blue Hydrangea

Hydrangea macrophylla

Blue hydrangeas symbolize deep gratitude and heartfelt emotion. Their color is uniquely determined by the acidity of the soil they grow in.
